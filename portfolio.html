Luca O'D Art Studio

My art journey began while I held a corporate job that required a lot of travel. I had always been interested in photography and took pictures everywhere that I went. I began to see different meaning in the pictures that I took from my plane window view and set about painting these. Gradually, I was able to work my way into being a full time artist. I started my art studio in 2018 and have continually added changes to make it perfectly suitable to my creative needs as an artist.
I am constantly evolving and bring that to my creative pieces. I hope you enjoy my selection of art on display here and I look forward to welcoming you to my studio!
