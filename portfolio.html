Gallery

My art journey began while I held a corporate job that required a lot of travel.
I had always been interested in photography and took pictures everywhere that I went.
I began to see different meaning in the pictures that I took from my plane window view and set about painting these.

Red Rock

This piece came to me from a picture that I had taken in Sicily.

Mountain Stream

Although I have named this "Mountain Stream", it is neither a mountain nor a stream.

Green Thames

That signature twist and turn that can only be the Thames.

Snowflake Lightning

Winter brings out all kinds of beauty, even imagined!!

Dockyard Negative

This piece was inspired by the view during take-off from a favourite city of mine.

Connemara

Ireland's beautiful west coast from the air.

Lavender

An explosion of paint that grew into a lavender plant.

Mercury

Satellite Storm
